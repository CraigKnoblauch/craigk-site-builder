.. _art-section:

:octicon:`paintbrush;1em;text-danger` Art
========================================

.. grid:: 1 2 3 3
    :gutter: 3

    .. grid-item-card:: 
        :img-top: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ea/Van_Gogh_-_Starry_Night_-_Google_Art_Project.jpg/600px-Van_Gogh_-_Starry_Night_-_Google_Art_Project.jpg
        
        **The Starry Night**
        *Vincent van Gogh, 1889*
        
        The movement captured in the sky through short, thick brushstrokes remains one of the most influential techniques in post-impressionism.

    .. grid-item-card:: 
        :img-top: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a5/Glass_House_Interior.jpg/600px-Glass_House_Interior.jpg
        
        **The Glass House**
        *Philip Johnson, 1949*
        
        A masterpiece of modern architecture, blurring the line between interior shelter and the surrounding landscape.
